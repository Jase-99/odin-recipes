Sugar Cookies

Description

These 3 ingredient sugar cookies are super simple, and this small batch is easy to make in under a half hour.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup vanilla sugar
  • 1 cup self-rising flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet or line with parchment.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat the butter with an electric mixer until smooth. Add the sugar and beat until combined. Beat in flour until combined.
  3. Using a cookie scoop, portion dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges of cookies are set, about 14 minutes.
  5. Allow cookies to cool completely before removing from the baking sheet.
